    About Us:


    Rocking Wok Taiwanese Cuisine offers delicious dining and takeout to Seattle, WA.

    Rocking Wok Taiwanese Cuisine is a cornerstone in the Seattle community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

    Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
