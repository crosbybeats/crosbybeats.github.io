    About Oliver



    Oliver Crosby is a musician and producer from Seattle, WA who blends the genres of electronic music, jazz, and hip hop into a fluid and captivating sound. With an instrumental background in jazz piano, electric bass, and drums, he combines his favorite instruments and sounds into soulful creations. Inspired by the ever changing scene of modern jazz, he continually varies his ideas and sonic touch to keep his music fresh and progressive.
